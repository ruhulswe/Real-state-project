Building Facade

Elegant and sophisticated front elevation designed by prominent architect/designer.

Facing bricks/decorative tiles, ornamental green patches and glass panels/blocks are typically used to enhance the architectural aesthetics of the exposed facades of the complex.

All road-facing balconies have attractive looking railing complementing the façade.

Concealed exterior illumination is used to highlight the façade at night time.

Entrance

Decorative and secured gate at entrance to the complex.

Attractive exterior lamps, name/address plaque and decorative landscapic features along the entrance area.

Guard’s enclosure with message window beside the entrance gate.

Reception

Reception lounge is sophisticatedly finished with floor tiles / marble.

Stylish and spacious concierge counter for receptionist.

Intercom telephone connection in concierge, each of the apartments, guard room etc.

Basement/Ground Floor

Car parking area, Sub station, Transformer room, Generator room, Reception, Waiting lobby, Guard room etc. are located in this area.

Driveways are usually covered with beautifully designed pavement blocks.

Adequate lighting arrangement in driveway, reception area & staircase.

Rooftop

An office/meeting room for the owners’ association is usually set up on roof top.

Built-in seats on rooftop for recreation.

Clotheslines on rooftop for drying wet clothes.

Secured gate with hatch-bolt at entrance to the open roof.

Other Facilities

Ornamental apartment number plate on each apartment’s entrance door.

Spacious main staircase with sleekly designed railing.
