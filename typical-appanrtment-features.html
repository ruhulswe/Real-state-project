Doors

Main door with decorative Solid Teak wood door shutter and frame at the entrance finished with imported Secured Lock, Check Viewer, Apartment number, etc.

All other door frames will be of solid seasoned Lohakath or equivalent.

All Internal doors will be of Teak veneered plain flush door with Mortice lock.

All toilet and verandah door shutters will have Teak Chambal veneered flush door with imported Mortice lock.

Wood textured UPVC doors in Maid’s Toilet & Kitchen Verandah.

Windows

Sliding windows with 5 mm clear / light tinted Glass with 4 inches Aluminum Section completed with rainwater barrier and mohair lining.

Floors

All floors except kitchen & toilets are finished with high-quality imported homogenous tiles.

Sanitary Fittings and Fixtures

Concealed hot & cold water lines with G.I. pipes/fittings in all toilets.

All other disposal pipes (soil, waste etc.) are also to be concealed and should have easy access to repair / replacement.

Very good quality sanitary fittings/fixtures in the toilets.

Contemporary wall tiles in soothing colors in all bathrooms up to false ceiling height.

Non-slip ceramic tiles on the floor of all bathrooms in matching colors.

Bathtub in Master Bath and bordered shower area in other baths.

Imported head shower & high quality water faucets in all baths.

4-pcs bathroom accessories set with matching surrounding color.

Mercury-coated high-finish Mirror in all baths.

Maid’s bath is finished with local ceramic tiles in pastel color. Low-down pan, local water faucet and a framed mirror are also provided.

Kitchen

Slip-resistant homogenous floor tiles.

Wall tiles up to 2’-6” high from the top of the kitchen counter only.

One high polished stainless steel counter-top sink (imported) with best quality mixer (local).

Provision for Gas burner inlet & outlet & exhaust fan.

Paintings & Polishing

Weather Coat (Berger) on the exterior walls & boundary walls.

Acrylic Plastic Paint (Berger/equivalent) on all internal walls & ceilings.

Brilliant White Paint on ceiling of kitchen and bathrooms.

All wooden door shutters and door frames to have French polish.

Electrical Fittings

Calling bell switch at the entrance door.

All bed rooms, living room, dining space, kitchen and toilets to have sufficient light points, concealed fan hooks and plug points. One light point is given in each verandah.

Power outlets to be provided for electrical appliances at suitable locations.

Designated space & suitable power points for Air-Cooler in Master Bed & Living Room.

Concealed connection outlet for TV in Family Lounge & Master Bed.

Concealed telephone connection points in Living room and Master Bed.

One intercom set with concealed intercom line to be provided in each apartment.

Any optional work or improvement proposal may be carried out by the Developer at an extra cost to the owner after negotiation/approval of both the parties.

In case of non-availability (or shortage of supply) of the materials mentioned in the proposal, equivalent materials in terms of quality and price available in the market are used. Developer reserves the right to choose the available equivalent materials as alternatives.
