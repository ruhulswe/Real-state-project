About Us

Berkeley Homes Ltd. is one of the well-known in the Real Estate and Housing Industry in Bangladesh. It was established in the year of 2010. During these 7 years of service, Berkeley has steadily consolidated its experience and expertise with many successful and unique projects in Bangladesh.

We believe “Success lies in the ability to adapt with changing times”. That is something we use as our strategy to anticipate that change and mould our services to meet the needs of our valued clients. Among the basic demand, shelter is the priority considering any aspect of the life.

We designed our building facilities for the peace seekers. We offer futuristic living places within the most suitable location both in and outside of the Dhaka city.

We firmly believe that our achievement will speak for us. Keeping this belief, we cordially invite anybody to pay a visit to our project before making important decision in life as we respect the values of humanity and try to maintain it with the blessings of our valued clients.

BHL always a step ahead by adopting new ideas, technologies and excellence in all spheres with access integrated qualified expertise span of management from the same field to create a brand in the Real Estate Industry.

The team at Berkely comprises of over 100 professionals, architects, engineers that were chosen on the basis of their excellent technical and management skills. Additionally, a well trained force of workers is also employed at the various projects. The dedication, skills, hard work and experience of the Berkely team is what makes it one of the famous Real Estate developing companies of Bangladesh.

Berkely is a company committed to quality design and construction. All the buildings of Berkely have been designed according to the guidelines stated in the Bangladesh National Building Code and each building is capable of withstanding the code-specified natural forces like earthquake and wind. Not only that, each building is equipped with and emergency fire escape to protect the building residents in the unlikely event of a fire. All construction materials and equipments of Berkely projects have a high performance rating and are procured with great care to ensure the highest possible standard.

BHL is born to build prestigious projects for its valued clients and its target to become dream company for valuable clients through honesty, quality and service.

The senior partners of Berkely started real estate business in 1996 in the New York (USA) and Toronto (Canada). Professional experience and quality of our construction has also provided us with the opportunity to manage a variety of real estate development projects for other group throughout USA and Canada. Having this western idea, experience and sense, the Berkely management introduced their best effort to make an significant contribution for real estate sector of Bangladesh where clients, quality, honesty, sincerity, and dignity are the main focal points.

Berkely is a member of the Real Estate and Housing Association of Bangladesh (REHAB)
